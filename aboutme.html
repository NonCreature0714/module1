My Story

In 1991, my father purchased a Pentium i386 computer, and that's when I started to love technology and use it.
Shortly thereafter, he purchased an i486, with an astounding 33Mhz processor I could overclock. I purchased Descent what felt like a short time later.

Mario taught me how to type.

I struggled with severe childhood allergies, but still learned ballet, theater, and traditional Okinawan karate.

Fast forward a decade or more, and I'm still knee-deep in computers, a US Marine (now inactive) and a developer. (Semper Fi!)

With $bash and Cisco's Tool Command Language (TCL) as my only lonely companions for years, I finally ghosted them and learned Python, C++, and Swift. (I picked up Android dev along the way.)
